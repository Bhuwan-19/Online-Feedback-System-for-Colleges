Submit Feedback





🧩 Tips for giving meaningful feedback:
  • Be clear and specific about your experience
  • Mention what worked well and what could improve
  • Constructive suggestions are more helpful than complaints
  • All ratings should reflect your honest opinion

📝 Why Your Feedback Matters

We value your honest opinion! Your feedback helps us improve our teaching methods, facilities, and student experience. Whether it's about classes, faculty, labs, or placements— we want to hear from you.

Please be thoughtful and respectful. Your responses will remain confidential and will be reviewed by our academic improvement team.
